The Story

She thought she married a man. What if she married a story?

It started as something to pass the time. October nights, a marriage that had already ended on paper, and nothing on TV. He promised her thirty days of horror — one story a night, the whole month of October.

She only made it through a few. Intimate. Chilling. Specific in ways that shouldn't have been possible.

The details matched real cases. The dates lined up. The victims had names she recognized from old news alerts she'd long forgotten.

She made a connection she was never supposed to make. And she couldn't stay in that house — or that silence — one more night.
